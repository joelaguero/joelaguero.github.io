JOEL AGUERO

COURSERA

Re-designing Coursera's homepage was a fun side-project. I have a strong interest in online education and wondered what Coursera would like with a "live-ticker" showing what people are learning, online, right now.

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

I selected a few design principles to emphasize in my re-design:

  • Breathing room
  • Aesthetic consistentcy
  • Communicate value

I liked the way Coursera showed how many people are on their website and found myself wondering what people are learning as well. Including some examples of what type of content users are engaging with right now communicates Coursera's value to first-time visitors.

Breathing room simply involved expanding the main banner on the homepage and complemented my second design principle of aesthetic consistency. This inspired a sleek, clear interface that prevents users from feeling cluttered or overwhelmed. Finally, I revamped the search bar so users can quickly discover new content.
